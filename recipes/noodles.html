Noodles

A bowl of hot, delicious noodles!

These noodles are delicious and feature the best in Guyanaese cuisine. In this recipe, we will use a wide variety of Guyanaese spieces that you'll love.

If you want you can even add some more spice if you like it hot!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vinegar
  • Adobo
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sweet Pepper
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Put Chicken to wash in Vinegar
  2. Put water to boil and add salt. Add noodles when water has begun boiling
  3. Start chopping up onions, garlic, and sweet pepper
  4. Cut up chicken and put on fire
  5. Add salt, black pepper, and Adobo to Chicken
  6. Add onions, garlic, and sweet pepper
  7. Add noodles to chicken
  8. Serve!
